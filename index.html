Key Features

High-Quality Synchronized Audio

Generating videos with high quality audios that perfectly match character identity, gender, emotions, pauses, and context

Data-Driven Lip-sync Learning

Achieving precise lip synchronization without explicit face bounding boxes, through pure data-driven learning

Multi-Person Dialogue Support

Naturally extending to realistic multiple speakers and multi-turn conversations, making complex dialogue scenarios possible

Contextual Sound Generation

Creating synchronized background music and sound effects that match visual actions

Limitations
Like all Gen AI models, Ovi has practical limits that affect when and how it performs best.
  • Video branch constraints. Visual quality inherits from the pretrained WAN 2.2 5B ti2v backbone.
  • Speed/memory vs. fine detail. The 11B parameter model (5B visual + 5B audio + 1B fusion) and high spacial compression rate balance inference speed and memory, limiting extremely fine-grained details, tiny objects, or intricate textures in complex scenes.
  • Human-centric bias. Data skews toward human-centric content, so Ovi performs best on human-focused, upper-body talking shots, with diverse motion range. The audio branch enables highly emotional, dramatic short clips within this focus.
  • Pretraining only stage. Without extensive post-training or RL stages, outputs vary more between runs. Tip: Try multiple random seeds for better results.
